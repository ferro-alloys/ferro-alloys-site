# ferro-alloys-site
Ferro-Alloys.com is an AI-powered global ferro-alloys market intelligence, trading, company directory, CRM, and conference platform.
Core business functions:
- Ferro-alloys market prices for all products
- News and paid reports
- Buy and sell marketplace
- Global company directory
- AI market analyst
- AI RFQ and sales email generator
- CRM dashboard
- Conference admin, including registration and sponsorship
- Admin backend
